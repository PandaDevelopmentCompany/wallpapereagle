What is wall printing?

In any room, there is a wall that you would like to decorate, or better still, with a bright, individualized image. This is possible with a robot artist: it will print high-quality images on any vertical surface. All you have to do is choose a picture or photo and the robot will do it all!

What is the maximum image size possible?

The height of the image is limited by the height of the ceilings. The minimum ceiling height is 2.8 meters. The maximum height of the image without mounting additional structures is 2 meters. There are no restrictions on the width.

How durable is a printed image on the wall?

We print with UV inks. This means that when sprayed, the ink is “baked” under a UV lamp. After that, the image is not afraid of moisture and is resistant to damage.

What is the difference between wall printing and wall painting by an artist?

The most important difference is definitely the speed. Our vertical robot printer prints on the wall at a maximum speed of up to 2Sq.ft per hour.

What can I print on?

The list of materials is almost limitless. The requirements are vertical placement of the surface to be printed and its relative flatness (5-10 mm difference is allowed). Plaster, concrete, brick walls, glass and wallpaper are also suitable.

How do I select an image for printing?

Contact us, we will definitely help you. We have developed an image selection algorithm that allows you to quickly choose an option that you will definitely like.

What are the limitations when printing?

When printing, it is not possible to seal margins of 35 cm to the edge of the wall (top, bottom, left, right).
